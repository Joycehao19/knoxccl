The Greater Knoxville Chapter of the Citizens’ Climate Lobby monthly meeting will be held on Monday, October 16, 6:25 – 7:45 p. m. at the Bearden Public Library, 100 Golfclub Rd, Knoxville TN. Chapter activities will begin at 6:00 pm and end at 8:00 pm, with the meeting formally starting at 6:25 pm. The meeting, will have a short educational unit about activities and progress of CCL during the past month, and will primarily be devoted to defining team action items for the Chapter. It was decided not to have an educational speaker this month in order that team coordination and objectives can be addressed.
